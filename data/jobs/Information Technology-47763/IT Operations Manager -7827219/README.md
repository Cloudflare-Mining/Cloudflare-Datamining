<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>We’re seeking a <strong>Regional IT Operations Manager</strong> to lead and scale our IT Operations function for North America. This role is accountable for the regional delivery of IT support and services, including Onsite IT support, AV Event Support, Hire to Retire, IT Service Desk (level 2 and escalations as needed), and Vendor Management.</p>
<p>As a manager<strong>, </strong>you oversee the regional IT Operations team (including individuals in Austin, San Francisco and NY), set priorities for your region, and ensure alignment with the <strong>Global IT Operations strategy</strong>. This role reports to the <strong>Sr. IT Operations Manager</strong>, and partners closely with other regional operations managers to deliver seamless, standardized IT services worldwide.&nbsp; This role is based in Austin, TX and requires being onsite and includes on-call responsibilities..&nbsp;</p>
