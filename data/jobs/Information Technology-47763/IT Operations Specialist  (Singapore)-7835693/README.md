<h2><strong>Role Summary</strong></h2>
<p>As an IT Operations &amp; Asset Specialist, you will play a dual role providing essential onsite IT support for the <strong>Singapore office</strong> and managing the end-to-end <strong>IT asset lifecycle</strong>. This position requires a presence <strong>onsite 5 days a week</strong> to ensure a reliable technology experience for local employees while participating in a global team of technical professionals. You will bridge the gap between physical office support and the critical oversight required for asset procurement, tracking, and logistics.</p>
<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<h4><strong>Onsite IT Operations &amp; Employee Support</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Onsite Resource:</strong> Act as the primary onsite support resource, responding to employee incidents and hardware/software requests.</li>
	<li><strong>Office Technology:</strong> Ensure conference rooms, shared office equipment, and IT systems function properly.</li>
	<li><strong>Event Support:</strong> Provide setup and technical troubleshooting for AV technology and meetings using platforms like Zoom and Google Meet.</li>
	<li><strong>User Assistance:</strong> Deliver professional service for device setup, account access, and secure remote connectivity.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Asset Management &amp; Logistics</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Lifecycle Management:</strong> Own the end-to-end IT asset lifecycle, from procurement schedules to secure disposal or recycling.</li>
	<li><strong>Inventory &amp; Audits:</strong> Conduct regular physical audits and inventories to ensure 100% data accuracy and policy compliance.</li>
	<li><strong>Logistics &amp; Shipping:</strong> Handle all shipping and receiving, managing the secure movement of assets between global locations.</li>
	<li><strong>Financial Optimization:</strong> Partner with Finance to forecast budgets and track expenditures to optimize asset costs.</li>
	<li><strong>Vendor Liaison:</strong> Act as the key contact for vendors regarding contract negotiations and issue resolution.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Operational Excellence</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li><strong>On-Call Duties:</strong> Participate in a <strong>rotating on-call schedule</strong> to provide urgent logistics and support, including weekends and holidays.</li>
	<li><strong>AI Integration:</strong> Utilize Generative AI tools to automate repetitive administrative tasks and analyze inventory trends.</li>
	<li><strong>Compliance:</strong> Partner with Security teams to follow access control, compliance policies, and environmental regulations.</li>
</ul>
