<h3><strong>The Role</strong></h3>
<p>The internet is at a crossroads. As generative AI transforms how information is consumed, the relationship between content creators, publishers, and the infrastructure that supports them is being rewritten. Cloudflare sits at the center of this shift.</p>
<p>We are looking for a Strategic Partnerships Director to lead our strategy at the intersection of Content, Publishers, and AI. This is a high-impact, visionary role focused on defining Cloudflare’s position as the essential toolkit for the "AI-Native" internet. You will be responsible for creating the messaging, tools, and experiences that empower publishers to regain <strong>transparency, control, and monetization</strong> in a world of LLMs and automated crawlers.</p>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<p>You will lead the charge in helping the world’s most influential content owners navigate the transition from a search-first to an AI-first ecosystem.</p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Strategic Messaging:</strong> Develop the core value proposition for Cloudflare’s AI products, specifically anchored in the "Three Cs": <strong>Control</strong> (over bot access), <strong>Compensation</strong> (fair value for data), and <strong>Credit</strong> (proper attribution).</li>
	<li><strong>Insights-Driven Strategy:</strong> Lead deep-dive research into the challenges facing premium publishers, using those insights to influence both marketing campaigns and the product roadmap.</li>
	<li><strong>Content &amp; Social Scale:</strong> Oversee the creation of high-fidelity collateral—from technical 1-sheeters and web experiences to social-first "sizzle" content that cuts through the noise.</li>
	<li><strong>Market Leadership:</strong> Represent Cloudflare at the world’s most influential stages (Davos, SXSW, Cannes) to advocate for the protection and growth of human-generated content.</li>
</ul>
