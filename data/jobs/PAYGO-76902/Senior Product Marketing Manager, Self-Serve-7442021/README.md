<h4><strong>About the department</strong></h4>
<p>The Self-Serve Team is responsible for Cloudflare’s PLG business that serves ~20% of the internet. We run growth marketing and product experimentation across all product categories including Application Services, Cloudflare One and Developer Platform, and all Plans such as Free, Pro, Business &amp; Enterprise. We work to grow the business by understanding our customers’ needs at every stage of their journey from Awareness to Activation to Expansion and Retention.&nbsp;</p>
<h3><strong>About the Role</strong></h3>
<p>We are looking for a revenue-driven <strong>Product Marketing Manager</strong> to own the Go-to-Market (GTM) strategy for our high-volume <strong>Self-Serve business</strong> (Free, Pro, and Business plans) focusing on <strong>Cloudflare</strong> <strong>Registrar</strong>.</p>
<p>In this role, you will act as the General Manager of the Self-Serve vertical's messaging. You will define how we position our products (focusing on Registrar) to millions of developers and business owners. You will do this by crafting an end-to-end product offering that showcases the multiple ways in which Cloudflare can benefit website owners, from registering their domain on Cloudflare Registrar through to building, hosting, protecting and accelerating performance for their websites. You will partner closely with Product Management, Engineering, and Growth to influence the roadmap and ensure our self-serve funnel is optimized for revenue and adoption.</p>
<h3><strong>Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Lead the Vertical Strategy:</strong> Plan, define, and lead the Self-Serve go-to-market strategy, focusing on attracting customers to start on Registrar and then expand their portfolio of products. You will act as the strategic anchor for this business unit, ensuring alignment between Product, Marketing, and Growth leadership.</li>
	<li><strong>Market Intelligence:</strong> Lead market understanding, opportunity assessment, and competitive analysis. You must understand how Cloudflare Registrar stacks up against legacy domain providers and how our application security competes with other SaaS solutions, utilizing customer feedback to refine our approach.</li>
	<li><strong>Customer Research</strong>: Understand our customer personas, document relevant use cases for each of the personas and build specific plans for how to market to each of these personas at each stage of their customer journey&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>Ecosystem Expertise:</strong> Demonstrate a strong understanding of the broader Internet Infrastructure, Developer Tooling, and Hosting ecosystem. You will use this knowledge to develop strategic feedback and positioning that resonates with a technical audience.</li>
	<li><strong>Full-Funnel Execution:</strong> Drive full-funnel plans inclusive of product positioning, competitive differentiation, customer use cases and enablement. You will partner with cross-functional teams to optimize every stage of the user journey—from the initial domain registration to upgrading to the Pro or Business plan and adopting add-on products.</li>
	<li><strong>Influence Product Roadmap:</strong> Partner closely with Product and Product Growth teams to deeply understand user pain points and form business requirements. You will represent the "voice of the customer" to prioritize solutions and influence the direction of product development for our self-serve offerings.</li>
</ul>
