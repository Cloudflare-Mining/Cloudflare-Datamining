<p><strong>Position Location: Austin, TX | San Francisco, CA</strong></p>
<p><strong>About the Role:</strong></p>
<p>Cloudflare’s Marketing team is seeking a <strong>Partner Marketing Manager</strong> to drive partner initiatives and campaigns across the Americas. You’ll work closely with partners and internal teams to develop marketing programs that increase pipeline and revenue. This role requires expertise in partner lead flow, attribution tracking, and campaign execution in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p><strong>What You'll Do:</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Develop and execute the <strong>Americas Partner Marketing Plan</strong>, collaborating with internal and partner teams.</li>
	<li>Plan and manage <strong>co-marketing campaigns, events, and digital initiatives</strong> to drive partner engagement.</li>
	<li>Track and analyze <strong>campaign performance</strong> using Salesforce and other tools.</li>
	<li>Advocate for <strong>partner marketing needs</strong>, securing internal resources and alignment with global programs.</li>
	<li>Manage <strong>partner incentives, budgets, and MDF funds</strong> to maximize impact.</li>
	<li>Work closely with Partner Account Managers (PAMs), Field Marketers and others cross-functionally to drive optimal program design, execution, &amp; results</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Key Qualifications &amp; Skills:</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>8-10 years of partner marketing experience</strong> in the Americas region.</li>
	<li>Bachelor's degree in <strong>Marketing, Business, or Communications</strong>, MBA a plus.</li>
	<li>Proven success in <strong>partner-led campaigns and events</strong> that drive revenue growth.</li>
	<li>Strong <strong>organizational and cross-functional collaboration skills</strong>.</li>
	<li>Experience with <strong>Salesforce</strong> and marketing tools</li>
	<li>Ability to <strong>work independently</strong> and manage multiple projects.</li>
	<li>Prior experience in <strong>SaaS, cybersecurity, or networking</strong> is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>This is a great opportunity to shape Cloudflare’s <strong>partner ecosystem growth</strong>. If you’re passionate about partner marketing and delivering results, we’d love to hear from you!</p>
