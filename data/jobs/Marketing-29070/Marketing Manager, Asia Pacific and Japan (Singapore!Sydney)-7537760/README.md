<p><strong>Location: Sydney, Australia or Singapore<br><br>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>Cloudflare is redefining how enterprises connect, protect, and build their business through our Connectivity Cloud, a unified platform of connectivity, security, and developer services powered by a programmable global network. As one of the fastest-growing enterprise technology companies in the world, with annual revenue growth exceeding 30%, Cloudflare is recognised as an industry leader, and continues to set the pace in innovation, simplicity, and user experience. We are seeking a passionate and strategic SASE Marketing Leader, APAC &amp; Japan, to drive adoption of Cloudflare One, our market-leading SASE platform. This role is part strategist, part campaign manager and 100% pipeline driver, responsible for collaborating and creating compelling, insight-driven programs that engage CIO-led IT, network, and infrastructure decision makers plus security teams across the region. You will partner closely with the Global Product Marketing, Field Marketing, Sales, and Channel teams to craft narratives, campaigns, and joint-go-to-market initiatives that convert awareness into measurable revenue.</p>

<p>Enterprise Buyer &amp; Customer Insight<br>Deeply understand the care-abouts of CIOs, networking leaders, and CISOs — from visibility and compliance to performance and ROI. <p>Integrated Campaign Strategy &amp; Execution<br>Design and execute full-funnel campaigns in all target markets targeting priority industries, segments, and strategic accounts. <p>Partner &amp; Channel Marketing<br>Work with technology alliances, MSSPs, distributors, and GSIs to build and scale co-marketing initiatives that create joint pipeline and amplify Cloudflare’s presence in the market. Enable partners with content, campaigns, and tools that position Cloudflare One as a growth engine in their portfolios.</p>
<p>Pipeline, Measurement &amp; Optimisation<br>Track, analyse, and optimize performance across the funnel using Salesforce, Tableau, and DemandBase. <p>Cross-Functional Collaboration &amp; Execution<br>Operate as a trusted partner to sales, PMM, and channel teams. Champion alignment across global and regional stakeholders to deliver programs that hit (and exceed) shared pipeline and revenue targets.</p>
<p>Regional SASE Thought Leadership<br>Be the regional champion for Cloudflare One for sales leaders, partners and customers and our connectivity cloud, translating complex networking and security concepts into clear business value. Ensure Cloudflare is represented at industry events, partner summits, and C-level engagements. Position Cloudflare as the trusted platform of choice for connecting and protecting the modern enterprise.</p>
