<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you’re adept at communicating the benefits of complex technologies in fresh, lively, original copy; can quickly interpret client needs; and work well under deadline, Cloudflare’s in-house Brand team wants to hear from you.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cloudflare is looking for a talented creative copywriter to develop campaign copy, web copy, videos, social copy, content and messaging </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">that will have a visible and measurable impact on our business and brand.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The ideal candidate understands IT buyers and technology (especially infrastructure, networking and cyber security), is a strategic thinker who can understand and write to a brief in a consistent tone, and an effective storyteller who can help customers understand Cloudflare’s value in a clear and engaging way.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reporting to the Senior Creative Director, the creative copywriter will collaborate with project managers, designers, </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">video producers, product marketing managers, and digital/social media experts, and engage with internal clients to understand their audiences and meet their objectives with creative work that meets the highest standards.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li><span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Express Cloudflares’s brand image, identity, voice, and unique position in the marketplace to business and technical audiences&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Translate client objectives and creative briefs into compelling concepts, headlines, and copy&nbsp;</span></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight: 400;"> &nbsp;</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Brainstorm fresh ideas as key member of creative team</span></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">&nbsp;</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Write and edit content for assets including digital, print, and out-of-home advertising, video, interactive experiences, and microsites, as well as email, event themes and taglines.</span></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight: 400;"> &nbsp;</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Contribute to messaging guidelines and tools to help marketers and communicators express brand messages consistently and powerfully&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Draft and refine creative briefs to help internal clients clearly articulate their goals</span></li>
</ul>
