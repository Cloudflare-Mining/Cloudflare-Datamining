<p><strong>About the Role</strong><strong><br></strong>We are looking for a Field Marketing Specialist to support marketing efforts across EMEA. The marketing specialist creates, delivers and executes impactful marketing programs to drive awareness and develops qualified pipeline for Cloudflare’s sales team — specifically targeting Enterprise, Field and Mid-Market accounts within the region. This position works closely with the EMEA Head of Field Marketing and wider team to develop and execute campaigns and programs designed to generate leads that contribute to sales pipeline, revenue and brand awareness..</p>
<p>The right candidate will have a builder mindset, a strong knowledge of B2B demand generation best practices, be digitally savvy, have a natural inclination to measure the effectiveness of marketing activities, strong project management skills, and the ability to drive program execution while managing the expectations of various stakeholders.</p>
