<p><strong>About the role</strong></p>
<p>We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Marketing Operations Manager to support our North America (NAMER) marketing organization while partnering closely with global Marketing Operations to solve complex technical and architectural challenges.</p>
<p>In this role, you will serve as a strategic and executional partner to regional marketing teams, owning campaigns, systems, and processes end to end - from intake through launch, measurement, and optimization. You will also collaborate cross-regionally to ensure scalable, consistent architecture across our global marketing technology stack.</p>
<p>This is a highly hands-on role for someone who thrives at the intersection of marketing technology, project management, and operational problem-solving, and who can balance day-to-day execution with longer-term systems thinking.</p>
<h4><strong>What you'll do</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li>NAMER campaign and operational ownership with global architectural alignment</li>
	<li>End-to-end project ownership, including intake, prioritization, execution, and delivery</li>
	<li>Marketing technology architecture, troubleshooting, and optimization</li>
	<li>Process development to support scale, efficiency, and quality</li>
	<li>Cross-functional and cross-regional collaboration</li>
</ul>
