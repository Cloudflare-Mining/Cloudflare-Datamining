<h4><strong>Description</strong></h4>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cloudflare is seeking a Pricing Strategy Director to bring our offerings to life through compelling pricing and packaging strategies.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will be responsible for developing strategies for our growing portfolio with a specific focus on our eCommerce and product led growth (PLG) activities.&nbsp; You will collaborate cross-functionally with Product, Sales, Product Marketing, Sales, and Finance to align our organization behind our strategies to solve customer pain and deliver value. &nbsp; A successful candidate will have a track record of delivering results in a fast-paced and dynamic business environment.&nbsp; This is an individual contributor role, reporting to the global head of Pricing Strategy.</span></p>
<h4><strong>Job Responsibilities</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Understand the customer journey from initial trial to smaller purchases online to larger purchases through a direct sales team.&nbsp; Optimize conversion strategies to drive overall growth (reach and revenue).</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lead development of pricing strategies, incorporating customer willingness to pay, product strategy, competitive differentiation, and financial analysis.</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Analyze sales date, customer data, product usage data, and financial data to uncover actionable insights and build a shared fact base.</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Align the company behind a shared business strategy by collaborating with cross functional teams, including </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Product, Sales, Product Marketing, Finance, and Operations.</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Collaborate with eCommerce team and Sales team to develop product led growth strategies.&nbsp; Test, learn, and optimize.&nbsp; Develop pricing strategies to drive this growth motion (e.g., freemium, free trial, usage-based, tiered packaging).</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Partner closely with Sales to understand sales motion and customer feedback in our effort to build customer value, reduce unnecessary sales friction, and capture a reasonable share of that value.</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Partner closely with Product Management to understand product strategy, product roadmap, and key capabilities.&nbsp; Support development of product strategy and roadmap with market research.</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Monitor and measure strategies to evaluate growth, uncover friction, and refine strategies over time.</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Present recommendations to leadership (including executive team) to gain alignment and approval of your strategies.</span></li>
</ul>
