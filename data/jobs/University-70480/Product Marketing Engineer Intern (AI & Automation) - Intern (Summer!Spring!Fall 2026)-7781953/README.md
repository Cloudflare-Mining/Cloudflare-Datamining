<h4><strong>Role Summary</strong></h4>
<p>Build the technical infrastructure that powers Cloudflare’s Go-To-Market (GTM) engine. You will develop AI-powered agents and web applications to automate and scale core marketing and sales functions. This role is for a high-agency engineer who wants to use a modern full-stack approach to solve real-world business bottlenecks, bridging the gap between strategic GTM needs and technical execution.</p>
<h4><strong>What you’ll get out of this internship:</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Generative AI &amp; Agents:</strong> Master the Cloudflare Agents SDK and LLM orchestration.</li>
	<li><strong>Full Stack Development:</strong> Build scalable, stateful GTM applications at the Edge.</li>
	<li><strong>GTM Strategy:</strong> Learn how technology directly enables global sales and marketing success.</li>
	<li><strong>Entrepreneurship:</strong> Own the lifecycle of a technical project from ideation to internal launch.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Technical Translation:</strong> Learn to communicate the business impact of technical systems.</p>
<h4><strong>Role Responsibilities</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Engineering GTM Tools:</strong> Architect and deploy production-ready agents using the Cloudflare stack (Workers, Agents SDK, Sandbox SDK, Durable Objects etc..).</li>
	<li><strong>AI Enablement:</strong> Design autonomous workflows using Workers AI and Vectorize (RAG) to automate technical Case Studies, Sales Enablement, and Event Messaging.</li>
	<li><strong>Rapid Development:</strong> Utilize AI coding assistants (Claude Code, Windsurf, or OpenCode) to move from concept to a shipped internal tool in weeks.</li>
	<li><strong>Technical Ownership:</strong> Partner with Sales/Marketing to identify gaps, then build, launch, and manage the internal lifecycle of your technical solutions.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Role Requirements (Must-Haves)</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li>Currently pursuing a degree or program in CS or Engineering with a focus on building functional systems.</li>
	<li>Ability to commit to a minimum 12 week summer internship.</li>
	<li>In office 3-5 days a week in Austin.</li>
	<li>Strong skills in TypeScript/JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS, with experience building/consuming RESTful APIs and working with SQL or NoSQL databases.</li>
	<li>Proficiency with Git/GitHub and hands-on experience with AI coding environments (Claude Code, Windsurf, or OpenCode).</li>
	<li>A self-starter capable of taking a technical requirement from 0 to 1.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Nice-to-Have Skills</strong></h4>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Cloudflare Native:</strong> Experience having built and deployed projects on the <strong>Cloudflare Developer Platform</strong>.</li>
