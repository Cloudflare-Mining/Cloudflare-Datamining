<p><strong>About the Team</strong></p>
<p>The Marketing team at Cloudflare is at the forefront of driving our brand presence, engaging our global audience, and supporting the growth of our business. We are a dynamic group of passionate individuals dedicated to delivering impactful campaigns and innovative strategies. Within Marketing, the Operations function is critical to our success, providing the infrastructure, data, and processes that enable our campaigns to run smoothly and effectively. We empower the entire marketing organization by ensuring efficiency, accuracy, and scalability in everything we do. As an Intern, you'll be joining a team that values curiosity, transparency, and a continuous learning mindset, where your contributions directly support our mission to help build a better Internet.</p>
<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>As a Marketing Operations intern, you will support our marketing technology stack, campaign execution, and data operations. This role sits at the intersection of marketing, data analytics, and marketing technology, helping ensure campaigns run smoothly, data is accurate, and marketing systems are optimized.</p>
<p>This is an exciting opportunity for a curious and proactive individual to gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced technology company. You will contribute to the setup, testing, and deployment of marketing campaigns across various digital channels, ensuring seamless execution. Your responsibilities will also include maintaining the integrity and accuracy of our marketing databases and assisting in generating insightful performance reports that inform our strategies.You will have the opportunity to learn about and support the administration of marketing technology platforms, helping to optimize our tools and processes. Organizing digital assets, documenting best practices, and collaborating cross-functionally with various teams will be key aspects of your role, contributing to overall operational efficiency. If you are eager to learn, solve problems, and contribute to a collaborative environment, this internship offers a unique chance to make a tangible impact and develop valuable skills in marketing operations.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Assist with the setup, testing, and deployment of marketing campaigns across multiple channels, ensuring accuracy and timely execution.</li>
	<li>Support the maintenance of marketing databases, focusing on data quality, integrity, and cleanliness.</li>
	<li>Contribute to the generation of marketing performance reports, helping to track key metrics and provide initial insights.</li>
	<li>Learn and assist in the administration of various marketing technology platforms (MarTech), ensuring they function effectively.</li>
	<li>Help organize and manage digital assets, ensuring they are easily accessible and properly categorized.</li>
	<li>Document marketing operations processes and procedures to enhance team efficiency and knowledge sharing.</li>
	<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with marketing, sales, and other teams to ensure smooth campaign execution.</li>
	<li>Participate in various ad-hoc projects, providing support and contributing to team objectives as needed.</li>
</ul>

	<li>Demonstrated analytical mindset with an ability to approach problems systematically.</li>

	<li>Proactive attitude, eager to take initiative and contribute solutions.</li>

	<li>Foundational understanding of digital marketing concepts and principles.</li>
