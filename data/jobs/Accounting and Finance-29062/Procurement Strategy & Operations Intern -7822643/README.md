<h3><strong>The Opportunity</strong></h3>
<p>Join our Procurement team for a comprehensive, 360-degree rotation across the entire procurement lifecycle. This isn't just a "support" role—you will be embedded in three core teams to understand how a global organization sources, secures, and manages its external partnerships.</p>
<h3><strong>The Rotation</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Sourcing:</strong> Learn how we identify vendors, conduct RFPs (Request for Proposals), and negotiate the best value for the business.</li>
	<li><strong>Contracts:</strong> Work with the Contracts team to oversee the entire Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) process, including intake, contract review and negotiation, signatures, and archiving.</li>
	<li><strong>Procurement Operations:</strong> Support the end to end Procure-to-Pay process, including requisition and PO creation, supplier onboarding, and invoice coordination. Learn how procurement policies and approval workflows are applied in systems like Zip, Coupa, and Oracle. Partner with cross functional teams to resolve issues, ensure data accuracy, and identify opportunities to improve efficiency and automation.</li>
	<li><strong>Digital Transformation: </strong>Gain hands-on experience in Digital Transformation by mastering how to bridge the gap between business strategy and AI development. This role teaches you to identify automation opportunities through real-world stakeholder interviews, translate those needs into technical requirements for IT teams, and manage a project lifecycle from start to finish.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
	<li>Automating workflows&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Automating spend analysis reports with dashboard</li>
	<li>Building an AI-driven "Vendor Scoring"/segmentation rubric.</li>

</ul>
