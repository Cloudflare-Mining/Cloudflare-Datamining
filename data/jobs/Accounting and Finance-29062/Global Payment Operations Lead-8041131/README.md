<p><strong>Global Payment Operations Lead</strong></p>
<p><strong><em>Location: US Austin</em></strong></p>
<p>Are you ready to propel the financial engine of one of the world's most innovative technology companies? Cloudflare is seeking a dynamic, forward-thinking, and high-energy Global Payment Operations Lead to revolutionize our Global Accounts Payable landscape. Based in our bustling Austin, TX hub, this high-visibility role places you at the helm of global cash movement, directly impacting how our financial infrastructure scales with our hyper-growth. You will act as the ultimate architect for our payment lifecycle—spearheading the deployment of cutting-edge payment systems and pioneering new payment methods across the globe. This isn't just an operational role; it's a transformative leadership opportunity where you will tackle intricate cross-border banking challenges, navigate complex global payment rejections alongside international banking partners, and serve as a vital strategic partner to Treasury and Supplier Enablement. If you are passionate about financial optimization, thrive on navigating ambiguous international banking rails, and want to leave an indelible mark on a global finance ecosystem, we want you on our team!</p>
<p><strong>Role Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Global Payment Execution:</strong> Oversee and execute all high-volume weekly and ad hoc corporate payment runs for the Global Accounts Payable department, driving uncompromised accuracy, velocity, and strict compliance with global internal controls.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Systems &amp; Method Innovation:</strong> Spearhead the implementation, testing, and launch of next-generation corporate payment systems and innovative global payment methods to scale financial infrastructure and streamline multi-currency delivery.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Banking Partner Collaboration:</strong> Work closely with our international banking partners to proactively investigate, troubleshoot, and swiftly resolve complex payment rejection reasons, structurally eliminating operational bottlenecks and friction.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Treasury &amp; Liquidity Alignment:</strong> Partner intimately with the corporate Treasury team to manage critical daily funding requirements and drive robust quarterly free cash flow forecasts and actuals analyses.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Master Data Governance:</strong> Collaborate dynamically with Supplier Enablement teams to mandate and execute precise updates to vendor banking master data, maximizing straight-through processing rates and preventing transactional risk.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Technical Optimization:</strong> Masterfully leverage and optimize our foundational financial architecture—including Oracle Fusion, Kyriba, and direct bank APIs—to continually automate, refine, and secure our end-to-end payment workflows.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Role Requirements (Must-Have Skills)</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>International Payment Architecture:</strong> Vast professional experience executing complex corporate payments internationally across a wide range of global jurisdictions, demonstrating expert command over regional payment rails, payment methods, and rigorous banking detail requirements.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Advanced Systems Proficiency:</strong> Proven, hands-on administrative proficiency with tier-one treasury and ERP solutions, explicitly including Oracle Fusion, Kyriba, and direct bank APIs.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Professional Experience:</strong> 5+ years of progressive professional experience within an international corporate accounts payable, global treasury operations, or high-capacity transactional finance function.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Education:</strong> Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Finance, International Business, or a closely related academic field.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Core Capabilities:</strong> Exceptional analytical capability, world-class problem-solving skills, and a confident, articulate communication style suited for cross-functional collaboration across all management tiers.</li>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Application Requirement:</strong> To be considered for this high-visibility position, applicants must submit an updated, professional resume.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice-to-Have Skills</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Direct operational experience working within major global payment processing institutions or financial technology giants (e.g., PayPal, Adyen, Stripe, or equivalent platforms) is considered a tremendous advantage.</li>
</ul>
