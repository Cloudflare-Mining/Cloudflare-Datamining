<p><strong>Available Locations: Austin Texas or San Francisco</strong></p>
<h3><strong>The Opportunity</strong></h3>
<p>Looking to transition from the classroom to the high-stakes world of legal contract review and negotiation? This role is dedicated to our <strong>Global Procurement Contracts Team</strong>, focusing on the legal framework that governs our most critical vendor relationships. You will gain direct experience in commercial contracting, risk mitigation, security and data privacy.</p>
<h3><strong>The Mission</strong></h3>
<p>You will lead a high-priority "Contract Health" initiative. Under the guidance of our Legal and Procurement teams, you will:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Audit top-tier vendors to ensure compliance with our latest <strong>MSA (Master Service Agreement), DPA (Data Processing Addendum), and DSA (Data Security Agreement)</strong>.</li>
	<li>Identify gaps in our governing agreements and launch formal workflows to remediate risk.</li>
	<li>Participate in negotiation strategy sessions for new and renewing vendors.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>The AI Innovation Project</strong></h3>
<p>Legal tech is evolving rapidly. You will explore how AI can accelerate the legal review process. This may include:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Using AI tools to perform "redline" comparisons across hundreds of legacy documents.</li>
	<li>Developing a "Contract Playbook" within <strong>Gemini</strong> to help non-legal stakeholders understand standard vs. non-standard clauses.</li>
	<li>Building an automated tracking dashboard for document execution status.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li>Review complex legal documents for risk and compliance alignment.</li>
	<li>Initiate outreach and manage communications with vendor legal teams.</li>
	<li>Provide regular status updates to the Procurement and Legal leadership.</li>
	<li><strong>Final Capstone:</strong> Present a "State of the Contract Landscape" report to the Procurement team, highlighting risks mitigated and AI-driven efficiencies gained.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Qualifications</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li>Currently enrolled in an accredited JD or LLM program (or equivalent graduate law program).</li>
	<li>Strong interest in Commercial Law, Privacy, or Tech Transactions.</li>
	<li>Exceptional attention to detail—you’re the person who finds the typo in the 40th footnote.</li>
	<li>Ability to translate complex legal jargon into actionable business advice.</li>
</ul>
