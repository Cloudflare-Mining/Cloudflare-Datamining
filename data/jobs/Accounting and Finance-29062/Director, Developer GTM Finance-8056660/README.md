<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>The Finance &amp; BizOps team provides the financial insights and strategic guidance to shape Cloudflare's growth, profitability, and investment strategy. We're partners who embed across the business — connecting strategy to execution, driving planning and analysis, and collaborating with cross-functional leaders to develop and deliver key priorities.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We're looking for a seasoned, dynamic senior leader to join this team as the dedicated finance partner to the Developer GTM organization, providing the financial clarity and commercial judgment the business needs to succeed.</p>
<p>The Developer Platform is one of Cloudflare's fastest-growing businesses, spanning compute, storage, AI inference, media, and a growing ecosystem of developer tools — delivered through both self-serve and enterprise go-to-market motions, all with distinct commercial models, cost structures, and growth levers. This role is for someone who thrives in that complexity: someone who can work independently across ambiguous problems, build the analysis and frameworks needed, and turn them into recommendations business leaders can act on.</p>
<p>You'll work across functions — inside and outside of finance — to ensure the platform has coherent financial coverage as it scales. You'll define and build the models, the cadence, and the relationships, using AI-native tooling as a force multiplier on speed, rigor, and insight — becoming the first call when the business is thinking through a deal, a bet, or a difficult tradeoff. This role reports to the VP of Finance &amp; BizOps.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Forecasting and planning:</strong> Own the developer platform and self-serve revenue forecasts — connecting usage trends, contracted commitments, and deployment activity to revenue outcomes and maintaining a forward view the business plans around. Drive annual and quarterly planning for the developer business, including target-setting, scenario analysis, and resource allocation.</li>
	<li><strong>Commercial strategy and deal evaluation:</strong> Support deal evaluation for strategic transactions — assessing deal structure, payment terms, and margin implications. Work with the Pricing team, Deal Desk, and cross-functional partners to develop reusable frameworks for consumption-based and hybrid deal structures. Provide the financial perspective on go/no-go decisions and partnership economics.</li>
	<li><strong>Product economics and insights:</strong> Develop deep visibility into the developer business at meaningful layers of granularity — across products, revenue streams, usage patterns, and cost structures. Partner with R&amp;D Finance and infrastructure teams to connect product economics to commercial outcomes, and surface the insights that help the business make better decisions about where to invest and how to grow.</li>
	<li><strong>Executive reporting and storytelling:</strong> Serve as the financial voice of the developer platform to senior leadership — translating complex business performance into clear, narrative-driven perspectives that shape executive discussions and planning.</li>
	<li><strong>Financial stewardship and reporting:</strong> Drive monthly close, forecast, and variance analysis. Own budget management and financial stewardship for the developer business. Understand trends deeply and surface risks and opportunities proactively — the business changes direction fast and finance needs to be ahead of it, not catching up.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Desirable Skills, Knowledge, and Experience</strong></p>
<ul>

High-growth, public-company SaaS or cloud infrastructure experience preferred.</li>
	<li>Intellectually curious and AI-native. You apply first-principles logic to multi-faceted problems, are not satisfied with easy or convenient answers, and actively incorporate AI tooling into your workflows to accelerate analysis and improve decision-making. We are building an AI-forward organization and the right candidate sees AI as a partner, not a threat.</li>
	<li>A builder mindset. You are energized by ambiguity, take ownership of problems before being asked, and are comfortable operating with blurred lines of ownership.</li>
	<li>Operating experience as an embedded finance partner to a senior GTM or business leader (VP+) in a technology company. You shaped commercial decisions, not just forecasted them.</li>
	<li>Strong fluency in consumption-based revenue mechanics, cloud and AI infrastructure economics, and pricing strategy — with the ability to work credibly across technical and commercial teams and build financial models from scratch for new and evolving business lines.</li>
	<li>Clear, direct communication with an outstanding ability to turn numbers into narratives that influence senior leaders.</li>
</ul>
