<p><strong>Role Summary</strong><strong><br></strong><strong><br></strong>We are looking for a strong candidate to join our team as part of the Finance &amp; Business Operations Organization. As a key team member of a high-visibility organization, this role will provide both technical and operational support to Cloudflare’s Global Commissions Tooling &amp; Strategy.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
	<li>Develop and configure Varicent compensation models and calculation logic. Act as the primary technical builder for system enhancements, ensuring all plans are set up accurately and on time.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Identify and implement best practice configurations and build techniques, optimizing for scalability and usability while ensuring solutions align with stakeholder needs.</li>
	<li>Plan and execute end-to-end testing (QA/UAT) for all system changes. You will be responsible for identifying bugs, validating fixes, and ensuring error-free deployments.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Review and analyze application performance, to provide enhancement recommendations and process improvements.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Provide risk assessment for new functionality and enhancements.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Build extensive solution / process documentation and enablement infrastructure, scaling consistent practices, documentation and desktop procedures for tool functionality&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Build deep knowledge around and leverage relationships to influence outcomes and prioritization of upstream system enhancements which directly impact ICM practice</li>
	<li>Monitor data flows between upstream systems (CRM, HCM) and Varicent. Troubleshoot integration errors and collaborate with IT/Data teams to resolve discrepancies.</li>
	<li>Task Management &amp; Delivery: Independently manage your own project timelines and priorities. You will be expected to balance daily support tickets with larger system builds, ensuring deadlines are met with minimal oversight.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Must Have Skills:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
	<li>4+ years in Sales Compensation, Sales Operations, or Systems Administration, preferably within a high-growth SaaS environment.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>3+ years of hands-on experience configuring Varicent (or a similar enterprise ICM tool like Anaplan/Xactly), including component building, scheduler management, and calculation logic.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Firm grasp of end-to-end commission processes, including plan document tracking, transaction crediting, complex incentive calculations, and dispute resolution.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Strong SQL skills with the ability to write queries for data analysis and troubleshooting. Experience handling large, complex datasets is required.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Analytical &amp; Detailed: Clear, concise communicator; A natural problem solver with high attention to detail, capable of troubleshooting complex data discrepancies and validating calculations before they impact the business.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice-to-Have Skills:</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Industry Context:</strong> Experience with a SaaS business model.</li>
	<li><strong>Cloud &amp; Infrastructure:</strong> Prior experience working in the SaaS, Cybersecurity, or Cloud Infrastructure space, with familiarity with usage-based or consumption-based compensation models.</li>
