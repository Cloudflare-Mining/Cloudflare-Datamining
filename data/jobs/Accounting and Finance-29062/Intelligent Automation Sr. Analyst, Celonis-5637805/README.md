<h4>Available Locations: Remote US</h4>
<h4>About the position</h4>
<p>At Cloudflare we are committed to excellence and innovation, not just as it relates to products we sell to our customers, but also for our internal processes and systems. We are committed to leveraging cutting-edge technologies to enhance operational efficiency and drive innovation. In order to do that, we are seeking a dynamic and forward thinking Intelligent Automation Analyst to join our team.</p>
<p>This pivotal role is centered around process mining and process automation, requiring strategic planning, thought leadership, and successful execution of cross-functional initiatives. We are in search of a meticulous and innovative professional who thrives on devising solutions. In this role, you'll be expected to seamlessly collaborate with external and internal stakeholders across functions. Your ability to think critically, chart effective courses of action, and foster collaboration across diverse teams will be instrumental in achieving outstanding results.</p>
<h4>What you'll do</h4>
<p>Automation Strategy and Planning:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Work with senior finance leadership to develop and implement a comprehensive process mining and automation strategy aligned with organizational goals.</li>
	<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams and process owners to help identify opportunities for automation.</li>
	<li>Evaluate and recommend suitable intelligent automation and process mining tools to help solve business problems.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Work with the business to develop thorough business requirements and proto-types as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p>Tool Implementation and Configuration:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Manage process mining / automation projects from information gathering to delivery and design, construct, install &amp; test.</li>
	<li>Collaborate with IT teams to integrate automation solutions with existing systems and applications.</li>
	<li>Conduct thorough testing and troubleshooting, working with business stakeholders, to ensure the effectiveness of implemented solutions.</li>
	<li>Work with internal teams to ensure high data quality and reliability of data for process mining and automation solutions.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Configure relevant process KPIs in process mining dashboards for internal ROI reporting.</li>
	<li>Ensure use cases executed meet business requirements and industry standards.</li>
</ul>
<p>Data &amp; Process Analysis:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Analyze raw data and help identify business initiatives from a process exploration perspective.</li>
	<li>Assist the business in interpreting trends, patterns, and anomalies based on insights.</li>
	<li>Identify, source, and analyze data and process maps from various platforms; verify data integrity while documenting data requirements.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p>Monitoring and Maintenance:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Establish and maintain process automation / process mining tools to ensure the ongoing performance of automation solutions.</li>
	<li>Proactively identify and address issues, optimizing automation processes for continuous improvement.</li>
	<li>Collaborate with the IT support team to troubleshoot and resolve any technical challenges.</li>
</ul>
<p>Documentation and Training:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Create comprehensive documentation for implemented solutions, including process workflows and technical specifications.</li>
	<li>Provide training and support to end-users and internal teams on intelligent automation tools and processes.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Examples of desirable skills, knowledge and experience</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business, or related field.</li>
	<li>6+ years of experience working in data analytics, data science, data-driven decision-making or statistical analysis.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>3 - 5+ years of hands-on experience with process mining technology, specifically Celonis.</li>
	<li>Experience leveraging automation and business intelligence toolkits such as UiPath, PowerAutomate, Alteryx, etc.</li>
	<li>Proficiency in data warehousing, data modeling and schema design.</li>
	<li>Proven ability to write data queries using SQL and Python.</li>
	<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to interpret and leverage data for process improvement.</li>
	<li>Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work across functional teams.</li>
	<li>Experience in process mapping, workflow design, and automation scripting.</li>
	<li>Knowledge of best practices in intelligent automation and a commitment to staying current on industry trends.</li>
</ul>
<p><em>Preferred Qualifications:</em></p>
<ul>
	<li>Certification in Celonis, UiPath, Alteryx, or other relevant intelligent automation tools.</li>
	<li>Experience in implementing automation solutions in complex business environments.</li>
	<li>Experience working and influencing in a cross-functional global environment with all levels of employees.</li>
	<li>Familiarity with machine learning and artificial intelligence concepts in the context of process automation.</li>
	<li>Experience in IT-Consulting, Management Consulting, Process Improvement or a similar area.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
