<p><strong>Our Mission</strong></p>
<p>The Security Response Team’s mission is to systematically respond to security threats safeguarding Cloudflare. We operate 24/7 across the globe to respond to security incidents, continuously improve our response capabilities, lead digital investigations and enhance our overall security posture. Our “Cloudflare on Cloudflare”, data and automation first philosophy makes us a cohesive team with high impact.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The Role</strong></p>
<p>As a Director of Global SIRT, you'll refine security processes, focusing on operations and incident response. Responsibilities span proactive threat management across edge, product, cloud, workloads, and containers. You'll lead a Global Security Response Team assessing&nbsp; critical security events through triage, cyber-attack analysis, scoping, containment, and forensics. You will help create scalable analysis processes and methods, build response frameworks, and collaborate with IT, Engineering, Product, and Legal teams. This role demands diverse technical skills, such as tooling development, automation, custom log analysis, a solid understanding of OS internals, and deep technical expertise in threat detection, incident analysis, forensics and SIEM systems.</p>
