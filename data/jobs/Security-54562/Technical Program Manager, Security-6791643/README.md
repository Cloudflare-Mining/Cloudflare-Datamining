<p><strong>About the Department</strong></p>
<p>Security at Cloudflare is a diverse, focused team committed to helping secure both Cloudflare and our customers.&nbsp; And the Technical Program Management team is the group that helps connect the dots between threats, risk, business needs, and innovation.&nbsp; We drive the transformation of security with program and project execution to reduce risk at Cloudflare while partnering to build the best solutions for our customers.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>We need to solve big security challenges at scale.&nbsp; As a Technical Program Manager on the security team, at a high level, you will help discover, define, and drive the execution of transformational security projects&nbsp; to reduce risk at Cloudflare. You will work with our team and all of our stakeholders to:&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
	<li>Build relationships across Cloudflare’s many teams and be an expert with communication&nbsp; and maintaining alignment&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Synthesize ideas and concepts into executable program and project plans and be the voice of the change</li>
	<li>Support the long term program roadmap planning, manage project updates aligned with the roadmap and report on project level OKRs&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Ensure decisions get made that drive programs and projects forward&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Drive alignment on capacity and resource allocation&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Identify stakeholders and drive alignment, including dependency identification and tracking</li>
	<li>Maintain the&nbsp; backlog of change initiatives and support prioritization</li>
	<li>Generate cadenced program updates, reports, and metrics that provide executive and board level summaries&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Lead conversations with key internal and external stakeholders to make tough decisions, define and prioritize requirements, and estimate work efforts</li>
	<li>Manage change to scope, timeline, key results/outcomes and budget&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Maintain a project management framework using&nbsp; milestones, communication plans, decision logs, issues, risks, etc.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Facilitate vendor procurement processes</li>
	<li>Close out projects with readouts on objectives met, sponsor and stakeholder alignment on success, and lessons learned</li>
	<li>Balance the focus on long term vision and roadmap versus addressing high risk tactical security issues</li>
</ul>
