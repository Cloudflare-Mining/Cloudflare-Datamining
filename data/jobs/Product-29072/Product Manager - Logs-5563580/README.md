<h4>About the role</h4>
<p>As the Product Manager for Logs, your primary goal is to enhance the utility and functionality of Cloudflare Logs — ensuring that they deliver actionable insights about both Cloudflare's services and the customer's own infrastructure.</p>
<p>You will have immediate responsibility for the Logpush product, which helps customers push raw logs to their destination of choice. You’ll be responsible for bringing our new storage and search offering, Logs Explorer, from beta to GA — growing customers, usage and revenue. You’ll also be responsible for Instant Logs, which helps users get real-time insight into their traffic. While your main goal is to serve Cloudflare’s end-users, you’ll also work with other product teams at Cloudflare to onboard new datasets and make sure these products have strong adoption internally.</p>
<h4>Key Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
	<li>Ownership of Logpush and Logs Explorer: You will be responsible for the end-to-end management of these tools, from conceptualization to deployment, and ongoing enhancement.</li>
	<li>Customer Engagement: Engage directly with customers to deeply understand their needs. You will be the voice of the customer within Cloudflare, ensuring that our logs products are continuously evolving to meet their requirements.</li>
	<li>Product Strategy and Vision: Define and drive the strategic direction for Cloudflare's logs products, aligning with broader company objectives and customer needs.</li>
	<li>Cross-Functional Leadership: Collaborate with engineering, design, marketing, sales, and support teams to deliver an integrated product experience.</li>
	<li>Data-Driven Decision Making: Utilize data analytics to inform product decisions and measure the impact of new features and improvements.</li>
	<li>Market Analysis: Keep abreast of industry trends and competitor activities to ensure Cloudflare remains a leader in cloud connectivity and security.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Key Competencies</h4>
<p>In this role, you’ll be responsible for building and delivering brand-new products. <ul>
	<li>Strong qualitative and quantitative reasoning skills. You can synthesize customer feedback with hard data about product usage.</li>
	<li>Prior experience delivering software products — whether as a student, intern, developer, analyst or product manager.</li>

	<li>Able to define and manage complex process and/or product issues of a broad scope using independent judgment</li>
</ul>
