<p><strong>About The Role</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for an Inside Partner Account Manager (IPAM) for ASEAN to join our Partner Sales Organization. This role will be based out of Singapore and will report to the Vice President, Partner Sales - APAC.</p>
<p>As an IPAM, you will assume a key position responsible for recruiting, onboarding and enabling a subset of targeted partners in the assigned region, while building relationships to grow Cloudflare’s partner business. Your key performance indicators will be growth of deal registrations, partner-initiated opportunities and partner-initiated sales in your region. To help you succeed, you will work closely with a broad range of team members who will support you, including partner ecosystem, sales, solutions engineering, marketing, and enablement. Your ultimate goal is to drive incremental partner-initiated pipeline and revenue through partners.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Activate a set of partners assigned to your specific regional territory</li>
	<li>Establish a cadence with a set of partners that you will develop and grow, including setting goals, developing partner plans, reporting metrics and tracking progress - with the objective of pipeline growth</li>
	<li>Lead demand generation initiatives and campaigns to drive partner-initiated pipeline in collaboration with the sales team</li>
	<li>Grow deal registrations, partner-initiated opportunities and partner-initiated sales</li>
	<li>Follow up on sales enablement sessions to ensure that partners understand the value proposition, pipeline generation marketing assets to leverage, and the associated SPIFFs / incentives.</li>
	<li>Coordinate and lead monthly sales and technical enablement sessions</li>
	<li>Train and enable partners on our technology and GTM strategies</li>
	<li>Coordinate account mapping sessions with our partners</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Desirable</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>3+ years experience working with partners&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Experience working in the technology, cybersecurity and/or networking sector is a bonus</li>
	<li>Understanding of cloud infrastructure ecosystem and cloud security is highly preferred.</li>
	<li>Confident presentation and interpersonal communication skills (verbal and written)</li>
	<li>Existing relationships and/or strong familiarity of the partner ecosystem in the region covered</li>
	<li>Direct experience in recruiting, onboarding, enabling and scaling various types of partners.</li>
	<li>Builder, hustle and grit mentality, with a bias towards action</li>
	<li>Entrepreneurial mindset focused on continuous improvement</li>
	<li>Naturally curious, considers themselves to be a lifelong learner</li>
	<li>Experience working in a fast-paced startup environment</li>
	<li>Technical competence strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
