<p><strong>Job Title&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Senior Partner Marketing Manager</p>
<p><strong>Role Summary</strong></p>
<p>Cloudflare is seeking a strategic, results-driven AMER Partner Marketing Manager to accelerate our partner-led growth through our most influential National Partners and Distribution ecosystem. In this role, you will be the strategic lead for our scalable marketing engine, driving the initiatives that fuel our mission to Help Build a Better Internet.</p>
<p><strong>Role Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Technical Enablement &amp; Hands-on Engagements:</strong> Program Manage our Partner Forums which are sales and technical series designed to move partner engineers from awareness to advocacy.</li>
	<li><strong>National &amp; Distribution Strategy:</strong> Own the marketing roadmap for a select portfolio of National Partners and Distributors, ensuring every campaign aligns with regional sales quotas and Cloudflare’s global brand.</li>
	<li><strong>Mindshare &amp; Enablement:</strong> Design "Marketing-to-Partner" initiatives—including sales incentives, SPIFFs, and "floor days"—to ensure Cloudflare remains top-of-mind for partner account managers in a crowded marketplace.</li>
	<li><strong>The "Better Together" Story:</strong> Collaborate with partners to develop joint value propositions that translate complex technical solutions into high-converting sales narratives for the modern IT and Security buyer.</li>
	<li><strong>Full-Funnel Execution:</strong> Manage a diverse portfolio of activities, from high-touch executive roundtables for National Partners to automated, "always-on" digital demand generation through Distribution marketplaces.</li>
	<li><strong>MDF Stewardship:</strong> Strategically manage and optimize the Market Development Fund , ensuring that investments in partner programs yield high-velocity, measurable ROI.</li>
	<li><strong>Performance &amp; Attribution:</strong> Act as the "source of truth" for partner-led ROI. Utilize Salesforce to track lead flow, manage attribution, and report on marketing-sourced pipeline specifically within the National/Disty segment.</li>
	<li><strong>Cross-Functional Bridge:</strong> Align closely with Channel Sales Leaders, Partner Account Managers, and Field Marketing to ensure seamless program execution and lead follow-up.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Required Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Professional Experience:</strong> 7+ years of experience in Partner Marketing, with a demonstrable track record of managing National Partners and Distributors.</li>
	<li><strong>Scalable Program Design:</strong> Proven expertise in developing programmatic, "one-to-many" marketing frameworks and turnkey campaign assets that drive demand across the partner ecosystems.</li>
	<li><strong>Industry Domain Expertise:</strong> Deep understanding of the SaaS, Cybersecurity, or Networking sectors. You possess a sophisticated grasp of the channel landscape and the evolving needs of the modern IT buyer.</li>
	<li><strong>Data-Driven Decision Making:</strong> You possess the analytical rigor to translate raw data into actionable insights and strategic pivots.</li>
	<li><strong>Executive Presence &amp; Communication:</strong> Exceptional communication skills with the ability to influence at all levels—from delivering high-impact Quarterly Business Reviews to driving engagement on a partner’s sales floor.</li>
	<li><strong>Strategic Agility:</strong> A self-starter who thrives in high-velocity, high-growth environments. You demonstrate the ability to balance long-term strategic planning with the execution of tactical initiatives that deliver immediate business value.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Data-Driven Decision Making:</strong> Proficiency in Marketing Automation platforms (e.g., Marketo, HubSpot).&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>Commercial/Mid-Market Segment Knowledge:</strong> Experience in designing scalable partner marketing plays and campaigns specifically targeted toward the velocity of the commercial and mid-market segments.</li>
	<li><strong>Creative Content Development:</strong> Proven ability to create compelling, high-quality sales and marketing collateral (e.g., white papers, webinars, case studies) directly or by managing creative agencies.</li>
</ul>
