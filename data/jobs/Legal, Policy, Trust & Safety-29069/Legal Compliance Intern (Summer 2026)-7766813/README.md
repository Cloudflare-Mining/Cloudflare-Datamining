<p><strong>About Cloudflare's Legal Compliance Teams</strong></p>
<p>Our Legal Compliance team is the <strong>architect of integrity</strong> within the organization. We don’t just monitor rules; we build the frameworks that allow our business to grow boldly and ethically in a complex global landscape.</p>
<p>We serve as a cross-functional hub, sitting at the intersection of <strong>law, technology, and corporate strategy.</strong> Our mission is to safeguard the company’s reputation by ensuring that every decision—from a local charitable donation to a global third-party partnership—aligns with our core values and international regulatory standards.</p>
<p><strong>About the Internship Program</strong></p>
<p>As a Legal Compliance intern on the Legal Compliance Team, you will dive deep into the core pillars of corporate integrity:</p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Conflict of Interest (COI) Management:</strong> Assist in reviewing disclosures and learning how to identify, mitigate, and document potential conflicts within a corporate structure.</li>
	<li><strong>Gifts, Donations &amp; Hospitality:</strong> Support the review process for corporate giving and hospitality requests to ensure alignment with anti-bribery and corruption (ABC) laws.</li>
	<li><strong>Third-Party Due Diligence:</strong> Participate in the "Know Your Partner" process, helping to vet third-party vendors and partners to ensure they meet our ethical standards.</li>
	<li><strong>Data Strategy &amp; Inflow Organization:</strong> Help manage the "legal front door" by organizing incoming requests and ensuring data integrity across our compliance databases.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>The Digital Innovation Project</strong></h3>

<ul>

	<li><strong>The "Face" of the Tool:</strong> Acting as the first point of contact for employees across the company, answering questions, and providing guidance on how to navigate the new platform.</li>
