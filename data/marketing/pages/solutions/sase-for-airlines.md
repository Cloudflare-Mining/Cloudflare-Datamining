# SASE for Airlines

> Clear skies for safe AI adoption

Connect and protect your nomadic crew, third-party partners, AI agents, and in-flight passengers. Accelerate modernization with a SASE architecture built on one of aviation's most trusted networks.

## Use Cases

### Safe AI adoption and compliance

Prevent sensitive PNR, payment, and crew PII from leaking to unapproved LLMs. Help satisfy EASA Part-IS, NIS2, and PCI DSS 4.0 mandates.

### Secure the connected aircraft

Overcome Starlink constraints without custom certificate installs on passenger devices. Segment passenger and crew traffic while preserving visibility.

### Modernize remote access & airport branches

Decommission VPNs for a nomadic workforce of pilots, cabin crew, MRO vendors, and partners. Treat every hub, lounge, and call center like a coffee shop with cloud-delivered connectivity.
