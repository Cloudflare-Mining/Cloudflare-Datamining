### The Cloudflare difference

What sets Cloudflare's network apart from every other cloud provider.

**Every service, every data center** 

Unlike providers that run different services in different locations, Cloudflare runs its full stack in every data center — no backhauling, no tradeoffs. 

**Single-pass inspection** 

Traffic is inspected once at the nearest data center rather than bounced between security layers. Faster processing, lower latency, stronger security. 

**Sub-50ms to 95% of users** 

95% of the world's Internet-connected population is within 50 milliseconds of a Cloudflare data center — most are within 20ms. 

**13,000+ network interconnections** 

Direct peering with major service providers, cloud platforms, and enterprise networks eliminates hops and optimizes every path. 

**Data localization controls** 

Granular controls over where traffic is inspected, encryption keys are stored, and logs are retained — meeting regional compliance requirements. 

**Resilient by design** 

The network is engineered to prevent the harmful effects of failure, not just prevent failure. Traffic automatically reroutes around issues.
