# FindLaw by Thomson Reuters

> #### "  Cloudflare is amazing... Cloudflare is such a relief. " 

FindLaw by Thomson Reuters uses Cloudflare to secure and accelerate thousands of customer sites. Learn how FindLaw leveraged Cloudflare to quickly issue over 8,500 [SSL certificates](https://www.cloudflare.com/application-services/products/ssl/) — and to achieve major speed gains with [Argo Smart Routing](https://www.cloudflare.com/application-services/products/argo-smart-routing/).
