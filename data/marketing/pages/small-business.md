### Empower Your Small Business with Cloudflare

Launch confidently and stay protected with our integrated security, performance, and reliability solutions

**Advanced Security Features** 

Cloudflare's Web Application Firewall (WAF) offers superior protection by analyzing incoming traffic, blocking malicious requests, and defending against OWASP threats like SQL injection, cross-site scripting (XSS), and zero-day vulnerabilities. Our advanced machine learning also tracks and mitigates sophisticated, evolving cyber threats, ensuring your business stays protected. 

**Optimized Web Performance** 

Cloudflare's performance tools, including Cache Analytics and , Argo and Automatic Platform Optimization (APO) for WordPress, help increase cached resources, improve site speed, and enhance core web vitals. Our CDN optimizes the delivery of your web pages for fastest page load times, ensuring a seamless user experience. 

**Easy Growth and Scalability** 

Cloudflare makes it simple to get started with our free plan and step-by-step guide. Our integrated solutions allow you to launch confidently and stay protected without trading off security for growth. You can also enhance your online presence with features like Content prefetch for Google Search and other advanced tools. 

**Easy Hosting Solutions** 

Cloudflare offers a wide range of tools for making it easy to host your web applications. Whether they're static sites that can easily be deployed to Cloudflare Workers, or Wordpress sites that can be migrated to our EmDash CMS — Cloudflare offers performance and easy of use, out of the box.
