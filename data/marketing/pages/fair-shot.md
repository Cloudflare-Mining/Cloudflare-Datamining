##  Project Fair Shot 

#####  Cloudflare's Project Fair Shot provides help governments, hospitals, and pharmacies distribute COVID-19 vaccines fairly and efficiently. Our Waiting Room feature allows organizations to manage high demand for resources, such as vaccine registration sites, by queuing individuals and allocating access. 

### Benefits of Project Fair Shot

How Cloudflare's Waiting Room helps with fair and efficient vaccine distribution

**Manage High Demand** 

Cloudflare's Waiting Room feature allows organizations to manage high demand for resources, such as vaccine registration sites, by queuing individuals and allocating access. This helps prevent technical limitations and ensures a fair distribution process. With Waiting Room, organizations can deploy it in front of any existing registration website without requiring code changes. 

**Free for COVID-19 Vaccine Distribution** 

Under Project Fair Shot, Cloudflare is providing Waiting Room to any government agency, hospital, pharmacy, or other organization facilitating the distribution of the COVID-19 vaccine for free. This helps organizations focus on vaccine distribution without worrying about technical limitations or costs. 

**Award-Winning Solution** 

In 2022, Project Fair Shot received a Webby Award in the category of People's Voice Winner. This recognition highlights the impact of Cloudflare's Waiting Room in helping organizations distribute vaccines fairly and efficiently. 

### FAQ

###### What is Project Fair Shot?

Project Fair Shot is an initiative by Cloudflare to help governments, hospitals, and pharmacies distribute COVID-19 vaccines fairly and efficiently. It provides Waiting Room, a feature that allows organizations to manage high demand for resources, such as vaccine registration sites, by queuing individuals and allocating access.

###### How does Waiting Room work?

Waiting Room allows organizations to deploy it in front of any existing registration website without requiring code changes. It queues individuals and allocates access to manage high demand for resources, ensuring a fair distribution process.
